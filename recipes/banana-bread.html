Banana Bread

Description

Type of bread made from mashed bananas. A moist, sweet, cake-like bread.

Servings: 24. Yield: 2 loaves. Total recipe time: 1 hr 30 mins (Cook: 1 hr 10 mins, Prep: 20 mins). Per serving: 258 calories; protein 3.2g; carbohydrates 40.5g; fat 9.6g; cholesterol 31mg; sodium 214.1mg.

Ingredients

  • 5 very ripe bananas
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup shortening
  • 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
